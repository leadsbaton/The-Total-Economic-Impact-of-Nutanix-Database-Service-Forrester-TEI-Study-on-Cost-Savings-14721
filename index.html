The Total Economic Impact™ of Nutanix Database Service (NDB): Forrester TEI Study on Cost Savings and Benefits

The 2026 commissioned Forrester Consulting Total Economic Impact™ study conducted on behalf of Nutanix examines the financial benefits of Nutanix Database Service (NDB), a lifecycle management platform that automates provisioning, cloning/refresh, patching, and backups for databases like SQL, NoSQL and vector databases. This study is ideal for database administrators, IT decision-makers, and developers in enterprises facing manual processes that slow innovation and increase costs. By addressing pain points like weeks-long provisioning delays and burdensome patching, NDB enables self-service operations, reduces licensing expenses, and frees teams for high-value tasks, leading to faster application delivery and improved compliance.
